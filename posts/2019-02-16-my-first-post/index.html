My first post ... and blog

Sat, Feb 16, 2019

git commit?

It has been a while since I wanted to make the jump and dive into the publishing my thoughts and experiments to the World Wide Web experience.

However, the most likely outcome is that I be its only reader. But I’d be content with that.

Still, I do believe that some of my tinkering and experimentation could be of interest for other like-minded people. I (would?) like to think it is the main reason of my going public.

But maybe the real reason is that the sentiment of leaving behind some form of legacy soothes the weight of mortality. As a matter of fact, the blog almost got titled Rust in Peace; and that was after an (imagined) iteration of using Memento Buggy. I have ended up with a more rocky title, for the sake of a sturdier initial punch and the improved pun-ctuation.

Anyways, enjoy1.
